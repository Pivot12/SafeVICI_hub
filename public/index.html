Why I build these

Fifteen years in the automotive industry — electro-mechanical systems development, systems engineering, and program management — taught me that the gap between a regulation and a shipped, safe system is where the real work lives. I'm now focused on that gap in autonomy and robotics: turning standards, safety data, and hardware-software integration into things people can actually use and trust.

These tools are that thesis, working in public. If you're building in autonomy, AV safety, or safety-critical systems and any of this is useful — or worth a conversation — I'd like to hear from you.
